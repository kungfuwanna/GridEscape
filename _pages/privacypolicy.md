# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated / 最后更新: 2026-07-11

## English

This privacy policy applies to the Grid Escape app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by jun ma (collectively, the "Application"). jun ma is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider".

### Information Collection and Use
The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include details such as your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, the pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and the mobile operating system you use. 

* **Network Connection and Data Synchronization**: To provide a seamless user experience, the Application requires an active internet connection to synchronize and back up your game assets, gold coins, and save file data to the cloud.

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies
The Application or its third-party SDKs may use cookies, SDKs, pixels, and similar technologies to support functionality, analytics, or service delivery. Where required by applicable law, the Service Provider will obtain consent before using non-essential tracking technologies.

### Your Rights
You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by the Service Provider. To exercise these rights, or to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, contact the Service Provider at kungfuwanna@outlook.com. If you are a California resident, you have specific CCPA/CPRA rights regarding data access, deletion, and non-discrimination, which can also be exercised via the same email.

### Third-Party Access and Data Transfers
Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to aid the Service Provider in improving the Application and their service. The Service Provider or its third-party service providers may transfer personal data to countries outside your country of residence. Where applicable law requires safeguards for international transfers, the Service Provider will use appropriate mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

### Data Retention and Opt-Out Rights
You can stop further collection of information from your mobile device by uninstalling the Application. Uninstalling will stop data collection but does not automatically delete information previously transmitted. You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting kungfuwanna@outlook.com. User-provided data is generally retained for the duration of use plus 12 months, and automatically collected data is retained for up to 24 months, unless longer retention is required by law.

### Children's Privacy
The Application is not intended for children under 16 years of age. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Parents and legal guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's internet usage and instruct them never to provide personal data without permission.

### Security and Data Breach Notification
The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the confidentiality of your information. If a data breach occurs that affects your personal data, the Service Provider will notify you in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

### Changes to this Policy
The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The Service Provider will notify you of material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy with an effective date. 

### Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact the Service Provider via email at kungfuwanna@outlook.com.

本隐私政策适用于由 马竣 运营的适用于移动设备的 Grid Escape 应用程序及任何相关服务（统称为“本应用”）。“马竣”在此被称为“服务提供商”。

本应用或其第三方 SDK 可能会使用 Cookie、SDK、像素及类似技术来支持功能实现、数据分析或服务交付。在适用法律要求的情况下，服务提供商将在使用非必要的追踪技术之前征得您的同意。

您可以申请访问、更正或删除服务提供商所持有的您的个人数据。如需行使这些权利，或在基于同意进行数据处理的情况下撤回同意，请通过 kungfuwanna@outlook.com 与服务提供商联系。如果您是加利福尼亚州居民，您享有关于数据访问、删除和非歧视的特定 CCPA/CPRA 权利，亦可通过上述邮箱行使该权利。

### 数据安全与泄漏通知
服务提供商提供物理、电子和程序上的安全防护措施，以维护您信息的保密性。如果发生影响您个人数据的安全事件（数据泄露），服务提供商将按照适用法律的要求向您发出通知。

### 隐私政策的变更
服务提供商可能会不时更新本隐私政策。服务提供商将通过在本页面发布带有生效日期的最新隐私政策来通知您重大变更。
